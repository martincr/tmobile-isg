Never get lost again. This great package brings you satellite navigation with on-screen and audible turn by turn directions - view a demonstration here. Full PDA functionality in a pocket-size package, the MDA Compact is ideal if you work hard and play even harder. It makes using Microsoft Windows Mobile v5 (Pocket Word, Excel and Outlook) software easy. It has an integrated 1.3 mega-pixel digital camera with 2x zoom and video capture. The minimalist design means it's easy to carry without damage. And it's triband with 128MB memory, Bluetooth, USB and infrared ports.
