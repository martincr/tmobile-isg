Set up voicemail

You should let callers know it's you or they might not leave a message

Q: How do I access voicemail?
A: Your voicemail should be set up and ready to use. Just press and hold the voicemail key ( or on your T-Mobile phone) and you'll hear any new messages followed by a recorded menu of options.
Remember: provided you're in the UK and calling from your T-Mobile phone it's FREE to receive messages and FREE to pick them up.
Q: How do I record a personal greeting?
A: A personal greeting lets callers know they're through to the right person. To record yours, call your voicemail, press 2 for 'change mailbox features', then press 2 for 'record a greeting' and follow the instructions.
Q: I can't get my voicemail?
A: If you're in coverage but can't access your voicemail, call 222 and listen to the advice. On pay as you go phones you must make your first chargeable call to activate it, and although it's free you must be in credit to use it.
Q: Can I use voicemail abroad?
A: Yes. All T-Mobile customers can check their messages while roaming. You'll need to set up a PIN (see below).
